Blade Runner

If Cyberpunk 2077 wasn't quite your thing, you can at least enjoy Grand Theft Auto 2013 by dressing it up in a neon aesthetic with this visual mod. Intensity ENB dials up the contrast between colours to 11, lending this ethereal, miasmic quality to Los Santos that’s drenched in atmosphere, especially when it also happens to be drenched in rainfall. The game’s lighting effects have been intensified too, meaning you’ll often feel like you’re in a J.J. Abrams movie just as much as a cyberpunk flick.

Los Santos zombie apocalypse

Simple Zombies turns GTA 5 into a mini survival sim, where players must survive an infested Los Santos by building shelters, managing their health and hunger, and recruiting fellow survivors littered throughout the zombie-filled landscape. It’s a fairly comprehensive game mode, and can be proper terrifying at times.
